About Us

Brought to you by TOM FRANCK, JOYCA GEERTS, KODI JACOBS & KILIAN ASNOT


Bottled Up is a POP-UP store started by 4 graphic designers. They have a unique concept, that you won't find anywhere else. Small bottles that have tiny minaitures in it. They are floating in water. It's a really beautiful thing to have. You can use it as decoration or to collect it. Our store is near the MAS museum in Antwerp and looks like a giant bottle!

Their graphical experience gave them the opportunity to create beautiful things. They took it to the next level, by designing their whole store and flyers. They also designed their own miniature shapes. At the age of 20 they are already very succesful and selling more and more everyday. You can visit us or order online.
